Bascplain

Bascplain is a web service analyzing the performance of the Scalable Capital robo-advisor using Baader Bank’s customer reporting.

It will happily show you charts of the NAVs, weights, daily returns and total performance of your whole portfolio and any matched sub-part of it.

Examplary charts

  • DAX vs. portfolio performance
  • Daily returns of the equity part vs. the whole portfolio
  • Weights of the different asset classes
  • Portfolio NAV–globally and for some continents
